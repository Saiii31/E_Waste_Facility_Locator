KNOW HOW TO DISPOSE OF OLD LAPTOPS

When your laptop is disposed of improperly, it can have a lot of negative environmental and personal effects. For instance, if you just toss it in the trash, your old laptop can end up in the wrong hands, leaving your data exposed.

Even worse, it can end up in landfills, leak rare earth elements, and cause damage to the environment and our health.

2. Sell or donate your laptop

Many different electronics stores and laptop companies have programs where you can trade in your old technology for a small payment.

3. Laptop recycling

Laptop manufacturers like Apple, Dell, Lenovo, and HP also have recycling programs, and sometimes will recycle your old laptop for free.
